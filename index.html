GDSC WOW is a community event organized by Google Developer Student Clubs to bring together students and professionals to learn, share, and network.

You can register for the event by clicking on the 'Register' button on the top of the page and filling out the registration form.

The event is open to students, professionals, and anyone interested in technology and networking.
